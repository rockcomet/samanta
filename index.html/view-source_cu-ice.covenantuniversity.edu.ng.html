<section class="av_textblock_section" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="https://schema.org/CreativeWork" ><div class='avia_textblock ' itemprop="text" ><p><strong>CU-ICE 2019 </strong>aims at addressing the key economic and development issues in Africa and beyond. The conference is focused on vectors conducive to economic growth and alleviation of poverty, famine and low growth rates<strong>.</strong></p>
<p>The conference will be the perfect platform where entrepreneurs, investors, researchers, policy makers, students and other start-up champions from Nigeria and beyond identify new ways of helping start-ups and existing firms to achieve stronger competitiveness and enumerate the roles of entrepreneurship in solving economic challenges.</p>
<p>At <strong>CU-ICE 2019</strong>, you will have the unique opportunity to:</p>
<ul>
<li>Enhance your visibility and present your products to a quality international professional and educational community audience in a cost-effective way.</li>
<li>Network with other professional, educational experts and technologists from universities, companies, private and public organizations and research centres.</li>
</ul>
